┌────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  EASIEST WAY TO START (Just 1 Click!)                     │
└────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

   📁 Open RETROPLAY folder
   
   📄 Double-click one of these:
      • RUN.bat          (Windows - Recommended!)
      • RETROPLAY.bat    (Windows - Alternative)
      • run.py           (All platforms)
   
   ⏱️  Wait 2 seconds
   
   🌐 Browser opens automatically!
   
   ✅ Done!


┌────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  EVEN EASIER - ONE-TIME SETUP                              │
└────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

   1. Double-click: create_shortcut.py
   
   2. Find "RETROPLAY" shortcut on desktop
   
   3. From now on: Just double-click desktop icon!
   
   🎉 Forever easy!
